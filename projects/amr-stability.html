02 — DEEP DIVE

High-Speed AMR Stability Architecture

LiDAR RealSense Sensor Fusion Control Systems Fleet Navigation

The Challenge

A 40kg payload moving at 2 m/s introduces severe mechanical shear and drift during emergency stops and tight cornering. At these speeds, even small instabilities cascade into chassis tilt, localization drift, and potential payload damage — unacceptable in a warehouse operating 24/7.

The Implementation

Built a custom tuning profile mapping predictive velocity to traction control. Integrated sensor fusion data to dynamically adjust acceleration curves based on real-time payload weight estimation, completely eliminating chassis tilt and improving grid-localization accuracy at top speeds.

Key Technical Details

  • Custom velocity-to-traction mapping using predictive control profiles for different payload weights
  • Real-time payload weight estimation via IMU and motor current feedback
  • Dynamic acceleration curve adjustment — heavier payloads get gentler deceleration profiles automatically
  • Sensor fusion pipeline combining LiDAR, RealSense depth cameras, and wheel odometry
  • Grid-localization accuracy improvements at 2 m/s — maintained centimeter-level precision during high-speed traversal
  • Emergency stop optimization — controlled deceleration instead of hard braking to prevent payload shift

Outcome

Completely eliminated chassis tilt during high-speed operation. The AMR delivers 40kg payloads at full speed with centimeter-level localization accuracy, zero drift during emergency stops, and no payload damage — deployed across multiple warehouse sites.
